What is RISE AI?

Your Intelligent
Career Partner

RISE AI is an AI-powered Career Assistant built on IBM watsonx Orchestrate. It leverages Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to ground every response in a curated knowledge base — delivering accurate, context-aware career guidance tailored to your unique profile.

Whether you are a student entering the workforce or a professional targeting your next role, RISE AI acts as your always-on career advisor, resume coach, and interview trainer — all in one conversation.
